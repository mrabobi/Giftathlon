Description

The ontology was created using Protégé and for the visualization was used OntoGraph and OWLGrEd.
The ontology is used for Santa’s application to pick the perfect gift for every child. The children are registered along with their hobbies, and their demographic profiles ( age, gender, location).
Based on the hobbies, gender and age, Santa picks the best gift for the child and the best way to deliver it based on the road and weather conditions and the location.

Objects properties

The objects properties are the following:

 

  • OwlTopObjectProperty
    • PersonProperties
      • hasLocation (Domain : Person -> Range: Location)
      • hasAgeCategory (Domain : Person -> Range: AgeDemographics)
      • hasHobby (Domain : Person -> Range: Hobby)
      • hasGender (Domain : Person -> Range: Gender)
    • DeliveryProperties
      • cannotTransport (Domain : Transport -> Range: Location)
      • requiresCondition (Domain : Transport -> Range: Condition)
      • deliver (Domain : Transport -> Range: Gift)
      • deliveredBy (Domain : Gift -> Range: Transport)
    • GiftingProperties
      • suggestedGift (Domain : (Demographics U Hobby) -> Range: Gift)
      • suggestedFor (Domain : Gift -> Range: (Demographics U Hobby) )
